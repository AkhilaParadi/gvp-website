Gayatri Vidya Parishad MLBT School

GAYATRI VIDYA PARISHAD MLBT SCHOOL was established in 2014 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located in Urban area. It is located in VISAKHAPATNAM URBAN block of  VISAKHAPATNAM district of ANDHRA PRADESH. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 10. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is Not Applicable in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. English is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April
